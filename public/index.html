FAQ

What is an nft?

A Non-Fungible token is a non-interchangeable unit of data stored on a blockchain, a form of digital ledger, that can be sold and traded.

How can i mint my nft?

Simply connect your Solana wallet through either Phantom Wallet or Solflare Wallet. Before you mint, ensure you have enough balance plus any gas fees to complete the purchase and join the community.

WHat is the launch date?

Launch date is when we will start the minting process on the website. Follow us to stay informed about the minting date.

what is solana?

Solana is a public, open source blockchain that supports smart contracts, including non-fungible tokens (NFTs) and a variety of decentralized applications (dApps). Native to Solana's blockchain is the SOL token which provides network security through staking as well as a means of transferring value.

Where does my nft go after i purchase it?

The NFT will now show in your Solana wallet and it is located under the NFT tab.
